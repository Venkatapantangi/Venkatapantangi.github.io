Academic background

I recieved my undergraduate education at Indian Institute of Science Education and Reseach, Pune (IISER Pune). I completed my PhD in 2019, under the supervision of Peter Sin at University of Florida, Gainesville. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Southern Univeristy of Science and Technology (SUStech). Please look at my CV for more details.

Research intrests

My area of research is algebraic combinatorics. I apply representation theory of finite groups to compute some numerical invariants of combinatorial structures. Please read my research statement for more details.
